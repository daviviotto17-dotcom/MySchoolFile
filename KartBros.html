FAQ

Why isn't the game loading?

First, check your internet connection and ensure Kart Bros isn't blocked by your network. Also, try disabling any ad blockers.

How do I play?

Use the arrow keys or WASD to drive, and press the space bar to use items. It's straightforwardpick your character, track, start your engines, and have fun!

Which browser should I use?

We recommend Chrome for the best experience. Kart Bros also works on Firefox and Safari, but Chrome is the only browser fully supported. The game is optimized to run well in browser on all computers, from school Chromebooks to the best gaming PC.

Are there more Bros games?

Definitely! Check out Football Bros, Basket Bros, Soccer Bros, Wrestle Bros, and Baseball Bros. You can find additional free web games at iogames.space!
